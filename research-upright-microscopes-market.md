Binocular models, with dual eyepieces, provide improved depth perception and comfort for extended use. Trinocular microscopes include an additional optical path, allowing for the attachment of cameras or other imaging devices, ideal for documentation and detailed analysis. Each type serves distinct needs in research settings, emphasizing versatility and specific functionalities.</p></p>
