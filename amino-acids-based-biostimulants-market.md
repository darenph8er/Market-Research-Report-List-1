<p><p>Amino acid-based biostimulants are categorized into three main market types: foliar treatment, soil treatment, and seed treatment. Foliar treatment involves applying the biostimulant directly to plant leaves, enhancing nutrient absorption and stress resilience. Soil treatment focuses on improving soil health and nutrient availability through application to the soil, fostering root development. Seed treatment applies amino acids directly to seeds, promoting germination and seedling vigor. Each type aims to improve plant growth and productivity through natural amino acid interactions.</p></p>
