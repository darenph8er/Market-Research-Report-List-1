<p><p>Inkjet marking coding machines are crucial for printing variable information like dates, batch numbers, and barcodes on products. The Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) technology sprays ink droplets continuously, allowing for high-speed printing on various surfaces, suitable for industrial applications. Conversely, Drop-On-Demand (DOD) technology releases ink droplets only when needed, making it ideal for high-resolution printing on detailed graphics and packaging. Both types cater to diverse industry requirements, enhancing product traceability and compliance in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
