<p><p>The bicycle brake components market includes various types designed for different performance needs. **Spoon brakes** press against the tire, offering basic stopping power. **Duck brakes** use rubber pads on the tire's sidewall. **Rim brakes** clamp onto the wheel rim, providing efficient stopping. **Disc brakes** use rotors for superior performance in all conditions. **Drum brakes** are enclosed, offering low maintenance. **Coaster brakes** engage by pedaling backward. **Drag brakes** are used for controlled descent. **Band brakes** use a band around a drum, common in lightweight applications. Each type caters to specific cycling requirements.</p></p>
