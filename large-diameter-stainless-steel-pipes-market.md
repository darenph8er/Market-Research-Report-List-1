<p><p>The large diameter stainless steel pipes market consists of two primary types: welded and seamless stainless steel pipes. Welded stainless steel pipes are formed by the longitudinal or spiral welding of flat steel plates, offering cost-effectiveness and flexibility for various applications. In contrast, seamless stainless steel pipes are manufactured without seams or joints, providing superior strength and reliability, making them ideal for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including oil and gas, construction, and chemical processing.</p></p>
