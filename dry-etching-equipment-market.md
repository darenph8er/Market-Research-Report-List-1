<p><p>The Dry Etching Equipment market comprises various technologies used for materials removal in semiconductor fabrication. Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) provides high density and uniformity, suitable for complex patterns. Capacitive Coupled Plasma (CCP) allows for lower ion energy and is often used for softer materials. Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) combines both physical and chemical processes for better control. Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) specializes in producing deep, high aspect ratio features. Other methods include various niche technologies for specific applications.</p></p>
