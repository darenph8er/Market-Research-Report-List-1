<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake and clutch fluids market is categorized mainly into DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1 types, each differing in chemical composition and performance. DOT 3 and DOT 4 are glycol-based fluids, suitable for standard vehicles, with DOT 4 offering higher boiling points. DOT 5 is silicone-based, used in specific applications, resistant to moisture but not compatible with glycol fluids. DOT 5.1 combines advantages from both glycol and silicone, providing excellent performance in high-temperature conditions.</p></p>
