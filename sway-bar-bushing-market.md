<p><p>The sway bar bushing market consists of three main types: metal bushings, rubber bushings, and polyurethane bushings. Metal bushings offer enhanced durability and are suitable for high-performance applications but may transmit more vibrations. Rubber bushings provide a balance of comfort and isolation, reducing noise and vibrations while maintaining flexibility. Polyurethane bushings offer improved handling and responsiveness compared to rubber, with greater resistance to wear and deformation, making them popular for performance vehicles. Each type caters to specific performance and comfort needs.</p></p>
