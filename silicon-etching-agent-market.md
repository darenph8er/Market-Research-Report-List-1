<p><p>The silicon etching agent market is categorized into two main types: fast etchants and slow etchants. Fast etchants are designed for rapid removal of silicon material, making them ideal for processes that require quick turnaround times, such as in semiconductor manufacturing. Both types are essential, catering to various needs in electronics, microfabrication, and other industries requiring silicon processing.</p></p>
