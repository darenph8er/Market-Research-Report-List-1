<p><p>Orthopedics Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are vital medical devices used in surgical procedures to cut tissue and coagulate blood vessels through the application of electrical currents. The market is segmented into three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which utilize a single electrode for cutting and coagulation; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, featuring two electrodes that enhance precision and reduce thermal spread; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed specifically for sealing blood vessels by applying heat, thus minimizing blood loss during surgery.</p></p>
