<p><p>The construction industry core drill market primarily features two types of drill bits: wet and dry. Wet drill bits utilize water as a coolant and lubricant, enhancing the drilling process by preventing overheating and dust creation. They are ideal for creating clean, precise holes in hard materials. In contrast, dry drill bits operate without water, relying on airflow for cooling. They are suitable for specific applications where moisture is undesirable, offering portability and ease of use in various environments.</p></p>
