<p><p>The cable window regulator market is divided into two main types: manually operated and automated systems. Manually operated regulators require physical effort to open and close windows using a crank handle or lever. In contrast, automated window regulators utilize electric motors, allowing users to control window operation with the press of a button. Automated systems offer enhanced convenience and are increasingly favored in modern vehicles, while manually operated regulators remain cost-effective options in various applications.</p></p>
