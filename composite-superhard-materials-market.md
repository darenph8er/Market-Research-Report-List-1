PCD is known for its exceptional hardness and thermal conductivity, making it ideal for cutting and drilling tools in various industrial applications. PCBN, while slightly less hard than PCD, excels in high-temperature environments and is particularly effective for machining ferrous materials. Both materials are crucial in enhancing efficiency and performance in manufacturing, mining, and construction sectors.</p></p>
