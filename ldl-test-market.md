<p><strong>The LDL Test Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LDL-C</li><li>LDL-P</li><li>LDL-B</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LDL test market encompasses various types of measurements to assess cholesterol levels and cardiovascular risk. LDL-C measures the concentration of low-density lipoprotein cholesterol in the blood, indicating potential heart disease risk. LDL-P quantifies the number of LDL particles, providing insight into particle size and density. LDL-B focuses on assessing the number of small, dense LDL particles, which are more atherogenic. Other tests may include advanced lipid profiling and genetic testing to further understand lipid metabolism and risk factors.</p></p>
