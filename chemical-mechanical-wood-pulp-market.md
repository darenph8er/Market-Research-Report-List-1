<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Wood Pulp (CMWP) market comprises primarily Chemi-Mechanical Pulp (CMP) and Chemi-Thermo-Mechanical Pulp (CTMP). CMP involves a combination of chemical and mechanical processes to process wood, resulting in higher yield and strength for paper products. CTMP employs a similar method but incorporates thermal treatment, enhancing fiber properties and refining efficiency. Both types cater to diverse applications in the paper and packaging industries, offering cost-effective and environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional pulping methods.</p></p>
