<p><p>The chlorine dioxide market can be categorized into two primary methods: electrolytic and chemical. The electrolytic method generates chlorine dioxide through the electrolysis of sodium chloride solution, offering a more eco-friendly solution with reduced chemical handling risks. In contrast, the chemical method involves the direct reaction of sodium chlorite with an acid, providing a quick and efficient production method widely used in water treatment and industrial processes. Both methods have their unique advantages and applications in diverse sectors.</p></p>
