<p><ul><li>LCC</li><li>VSC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVDC Converter Transformers Market primarily consists of two main types: Line Commutated Converter (LCC) and Voltage Source Converter (VSC). LCC systems use thyristors and require large transformers to manage reactive power, making them suitable for long-distance bulk power transmission. In contrast, VSC systems utilize IGBTs, allowing for more flexible control, integration of renewable energy sources, and manageable at lower transmission distances. Each type serves distinct applications and offers unique benefits, shaping the future of efficient energy transfer.</p></p>
