<p><p>The kosher foods market is categorized into three main types: Kosher Pareve, Kosher Meat, and Kosher Dairy. Kosher Pareve foods are neutral, containing no meat or dairy, making them versatile for mixed meals. Kosher Meat includes products derived from animals that meet specific slaughtering standards, often subject to additional regulations. Kosher Dairy encompasses milk and dairy products that come from kosher animals and maintain strict separation from meat items. Each type adheres to Jewish dietary laws, enhancing market appeal to observant consumers.</p></p>
