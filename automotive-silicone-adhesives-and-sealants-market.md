<p><p>The automotive silicone adhesives and sealants market is divided into two main types: single component and double component. Single component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to moisture, making them convenient for various applications. In contrast, double component adhesives require mixing two separate components before application, resulting in stronger bonds with enhanced performance characteristics. Both types are crucial in automotive manufacturing and repair, providing reliable bonding and sealing solutions for components exposed to harsh conditions.</p></p>
