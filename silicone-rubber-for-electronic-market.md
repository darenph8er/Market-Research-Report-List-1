<p><p>Silicone rubber is a versatile material used in the electronic market primarily categorized into two types: RTV (Room Temperature Vulcanizing) and HTV (High-Temperature Vulcanizing). RTV silicone rubber is cured at room temperature, making it ideal for sealing and coating applications, while HTV silicone requires elevated temperatures for processing and is often used in high-performance applications such as molds and insulation. Both types offer excellent electrical insulation, thermal stability, and resistance to moisture, enhancing the reliability of electronic components.</p></p>
