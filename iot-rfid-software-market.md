<p><strong>The IoT RFID Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID software market consists of two primary types: passive RFID and active RFID. Passive RFID systems utilize tags that do not have a power source and rely on the reader for energy, making them cost-effective and ideal for tracking items in supply chains. In contrast, active RFID systems contain built-in power sources, allowing for longer range communication and real-time tracking, often used in asset management and high-value item monitoring. Both types enhance operational efficiency and data accuracy in various industries.</p></p>
