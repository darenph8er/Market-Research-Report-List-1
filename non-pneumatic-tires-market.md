<p><p>Non-pneumatic tires (NPTs) are airless tires designed to eliminate the need for air pressure, offering durability, reduced maintenance, and enhanced safety. Unlike traditional pneumatic tires, NPTs use advanced materials and innovative structures, such as honeycomb or spoke designs, to provide load-bearing capabilities and shock absorption. These tires are increasingly used in applications like military vehicles, construction equipment, and bicycles, with growing interest in electric vehicles and autonomous mobility.</p><p>The Non-Pneumatic Tires Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period.
