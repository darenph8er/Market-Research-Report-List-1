<p><p>The Industrial X-ray Inspection Systems market primarily includes two key types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). DR utilizes digital sensors to capture X-ray images, offering rapid analysis and high-quality results for detecting flaws in materials and components. In contrast, CT combines multiple X-ray images to create detailed cross-sectional views, allowing for comprehensive 3D assessments of internal structures. Both technologies enhance defect detection and material characterization, crucial for quality control in manufacturing and maintenance processes across various industries.</p></p>
