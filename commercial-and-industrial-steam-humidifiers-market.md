<p><p>The Commercial and Industrial Steam Humidifiers market is primarily categorized into two types: Electrode Type and Resistive Type. Electrode Type humidifiers use electrical electrodes to boil water, producing steam efficiently but requiring mineral management. In contrast, Resistive Type humidifiers utilize heating elements to generate steam, providing precise humidity control and higher capacity options. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal humidity levels in various commercial and industrial environments, enhancing comfort and product quality while ensuring energy efficiency.</p></p>
