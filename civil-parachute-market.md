<p><p>The civil parachute market comprises several types, each designed for specific applications. Ram-air parachutes feature a rectangular shape, providing better control and stability, making them ideal for skydiving and precision landing. Cruciform parachutes are cross-shaped, offering stability and control during descent, often used for cargo drops. Round parachutes, the traditional design, are simple and reliable, predominantly utilized for recreational purposes. Additionally, the "Others" category includes specialized parachutes for unique applications, such as military or emergency uses, reflecting diverse industry needs.</p></p>
