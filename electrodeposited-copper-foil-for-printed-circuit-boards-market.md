<p><p>The electrodeposited copper foil market for printed circuit boards (PCBs) is categorized into three types based on thickness: Below 20 μm, 20-50 μm, and Above 50 μm. Foils below 20 μm are typically used in high-density interconnect (HDI) PCBs, enhancing miniaturization. The 20-50 μm range caters to standard PCBs, providing a balance between performance and manufacturability. Foils above 50 μm are utilized for heavy copper applications, such as in power electronics, where higher current carrying capacity is required.</p></p>
