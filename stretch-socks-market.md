<p><p>The stretch socks market encompasses various types, notably gradient socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks provide graduated compression, increasing from the ankle to the calf, enhancing blood circulation and supporting muscle recovery. These are often used by athletes and individuals with circulatory issues. Anti-embolism socks, on the other hand, are designed primarily for patients at risk of deep vein thrombosis. They promote venous return during prolonged periods of immobility, making them essential in medical settings and for post-surgical care.</p></p>
