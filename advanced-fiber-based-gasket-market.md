<p><p>The advanced fiber-based gasket market comprises three main types: fiberglass, aramid fiber, and carbon fiber. Fiberglass gaskets offer excellent thermal resistance and durability, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. Aramid fiber gaskets are known for their strength and resistance to chemicals, providing effective sealing in demanding conditions. Carbon fiber gaskets provide exceptional thermal stability and lightweight characteristics, making them ideal for specialized applications in aerospace and automotive industries. Each type serves distinct industrial needs through unique material properties.</p></p>
