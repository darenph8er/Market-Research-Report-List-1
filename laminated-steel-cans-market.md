<p><p>The laminated steel cans market is primarily divided into two types: 2-piece cans and 3-piece cans. </p><p>2-piece cans, made from a single sheet of steel, are lighter and more efficient in production, often used for beverages and food products. Each type offers unique advantages in strength, cost, and design flexibility.</p></p>
