Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) devices provide real-time visualization of vascular structures. Guide wire-based assessment tools, such as fractional flow reserve (FFR) devices, evaluate blood flow in coronary arteries. Intracardiac echocardiography (ICE) devices enable detailed heart imaging from within the chambers. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) offers high-resolution imaging, while robotic-assisted surgical devices enhance precision in cardiac procedures, transforming patient care in cardiology.</p></p>
