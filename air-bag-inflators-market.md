<p><strong>The Air Bag Inflators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrotechnic Inflators</li><li>Stored Gas Inflators</li><li>Hybrid Inflators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airbag inflators market comprises three primary types: pyrotechnic inflators, stored gas inflators, and hybrid inflators. Pyrotechnic inflators use combustion of propellant materials to generate gas rapidly during a crash, ensuring quick deployment. Stored gas inflators utilize compressed gases, delivering a more controlled inflation process. Hybrid inflators combine both methods, offering the benefits of rapid deployment and controlled inflation. This diversity allows manufacturers to optimize safety features and performance in various automotive applications, enhancing occupant protection.</p></p>
