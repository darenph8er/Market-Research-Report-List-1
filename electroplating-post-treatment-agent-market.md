<p><p>The electroplating post-treatment agent market is categorized into three types based on pH levels: acidic, neutral, and alkaline. Acidic agents are used to enhance metal adhesion and finish quality through targeted surface reactions. Neutral agents stabilize pH, offering versatility for various substrates without aggressive chemical interactions. Alkaline agents improve surface properties by facilitating the deposition of specific metals and enhancing corrosion resistance. Each type caters to different plating processes, optimizing performance and end-product durability.</p></p>
