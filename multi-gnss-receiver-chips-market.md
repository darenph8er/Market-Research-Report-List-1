<p><p>The Multi GNSS Receiver Chips market is segmented into single and dual frequency types. Single frequency receivers operate on a single frequency band, primarily providing basic positioning data, making them cost-effective for applications like consumer electronics. In contrast, dual frequency receivers utilize two frequency bands, enhancing accuracy and reliability by mitigating errors such as ionospheric distortion. This advanced capability makes dual frequency receivers ideal for applications demanding high precision, such as surveying, agriculture, and autonomous vehicles, thereby expanding their market appeal.</p></p>
