Data Pushers actively transmit data to a central location or cloud in real-time, ensuring immediate accessibility for monitoring and decision-making. Data Pullers, on the other hand, retrieve data from various sources upon demand, allowing users to access specific information when needed. Together, these types facilitate efficient data collection and management in diverse applications.</p></p>
