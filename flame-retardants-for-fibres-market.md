<p><p>The flame retardants for fibres market is categorized into three main types: phosphorous flame retardants, halogen flame retardants, and other market segments. Phosphorous flame retardants work by promoting char formation and reducing flammability, making them effective for various textiles. Halogen flame retardants release halogen gases upon combustion, which inhibit flame spread but can pose environmental concerns. Other market segments may include alternative compounds such as nitrogen-based and mineral-based flame retardants, offering varied properties and applications in fire safety.</p></p>
