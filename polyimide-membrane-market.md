<p><ul><li>Both Benzenoid</li><li>Biphenyl Polyimide Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide membrane market is categorized into two main types: benzenoid and biphenyl polyimide membranes. Benzenoid polyimide membranes are derived from aromatic monomers and exhibit excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties, making them suitable for various applications in electronics and aerospace. Biphenyl polyimide membranes, featuring biphenyl units, offer enhanced permeation properties and are often utilized in gas separation and filtration processes due to their superior selectivity and flexibility. Both types cater to advanced industrial needs.</p></p>
