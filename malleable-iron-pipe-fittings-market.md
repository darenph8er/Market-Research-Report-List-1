<p><p>The malleable iron pipe fittings market offers various types, including coupling fittings, elbow fittings, tee fittings, and other specialized fittings. Coupling fittings connect two pipe sections, ensuring a secure junction. Elbow fittings facilitate directional changes in piping, typically at a 45 or 90-degree angle. Tee fittings allow for branching off in multiple directions, creating a T-shape. Other fittings encompass various designs tailored for unique applications such as reducers, caps, and plugs, enhancing system versatility and functionality in plumbing and industrial setups.</p></p>
