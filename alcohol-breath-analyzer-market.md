<p><p>The alcohol breath analyzer market is primarily segmented into three types: fuel cell, semiconductor, and infrared. Fuel cell analyzers use an electrochemical reaction to accurately measure alcohol concentration, providing reliable and precise readings. Semiconductor analyzers utilize a sensor that responds to alcohol molecules in the breath, often at a lower cost but with less precision. Infrared analyzers employ light absorption techniques to detect alcohol levels, offering non-contact measurement and high accuracy, especially in professional settings. Each type serves distinct applications and user needs.</p></p>
