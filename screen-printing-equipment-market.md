<p><ul><li>Flat-Bed Screen Printing Equipment</li><li>Cylinder Screen Printing Equipment</li><li>Rotary Screen Printing Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screen printing equipment market is categorized into three main types: flat-bed, cylinder, and rotary screen printing equipment. Flat-bed screen printing utilizes a flat surface for printing designs, ideal for large-scale graphics and industrial applications. Cylinder screen printing is designed for printing on cylindrical objects, such as bottles and cans, ensuring uniform coverage. Rotary screen printing involves a continuous process with rotating screens, suitable for high-volume production and providing consistent quality across various substrates, making it popular in textile and label printing.</p></p>
