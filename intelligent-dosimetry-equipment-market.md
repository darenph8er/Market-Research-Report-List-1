<p><p>The Intelligent Dosimetry Equipment market encompasses various technologies, primarily focusing on Thermoluminescent Dosimetry (TLD) and Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL). TLD utilizes materials that emit light when heated, allowing for the measurement of ionizing radiation exposure. OSL, on the other hand, employs light to stimulate luminescent materials, providing a readout of past radiation doses. Both methods are crucial for personal and environmental safety in healthcare, nuclear energy, and research sectors, ensuring accurate monitoring and assessment of radiation exposure.</p></p>
