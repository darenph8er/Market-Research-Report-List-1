<p><strong>The Automotive Air Intake Resonator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Line Resonators</li><li>Side Branch Resonators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air intake resonator market is primarily segmented into two types: in-line resonators and side branch resonators. In-line resonators are integrated directly into the air intake system, designed to reduce noise levels and improve airflow efficiency. Conversely, side branch resonators are auxiliary components that branch off from the main intake, specifically tuned to target and eliminate specific noise frequencies. Both types enhance engine performance and acoustics, contributing to a more refined driving experience.</p></p>
