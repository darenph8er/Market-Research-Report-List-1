<p><ul><li>Vehicle Automatic Understanding System</li><li>Short Range Communication</li><li>Global Position Finding Satellite System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) systems consist of various market types, including Vehicle Automatic Understanding Systems, which use onboard units to identify and charge vehicles automatically. Short Range Communication systems facilitate real-time data exchange between vehicles and toll infrastructure, enhancing efficiency. Global Positioning Satellite Systems utilize satellite signals to determine vehicle locations for precise tolling based on distance traveled. Collectively, these technologies streamline toll collection processes, improve traffic flow, and reduce the need for physical toll booths, enhancing overall transportation efficiency.</p></p>
