<p><p>The bimetallic sensor market is divided into two main types: analog and digital. Analog bimetallic sensors operate using mechanical movement to measure temperature changes, providing continuous output proportional to temperature variations. In contrast, digital bimetallic sensors convert temperature readings into digital signals, allowing for more precise measurements and easier integration with modern electronic systems. Both types serve various industries, including HVAC, automotive, and manufacturing, facilitating temperature monitoring and control based on their differing response mechanisms and applications.</p></p>
