<p><strong>The Bone Densitometer System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Bone Densitometer</li><li>Peripheral Bone Densitometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone densitometer system market is primarily categorized into two types: axial and peripheral bone densitometers. Axial bone densitometers primarily assess bone density in the spine and hips, providing comprehensive information for diagnosing osteoporosis. Peripheral bone densitometers, on the other hand, evaluate bone density in extremities such as wrists and fingers, offering a quicker and more portable assessment option. Both types serve essential roles in evaluating bone health, guiding treatment decisions, and monitoring therapy effectiveness in patients at risk of fractures.</p></p>
