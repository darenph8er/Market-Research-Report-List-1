<p><p>The Paint and Urethane Stripper market is categorized into alkaline and acidic types. Alkaline strippers utilize a high pH formulation, effectively breaking down paint and coatings through saponification, making them suitable for various surfaces without damaging underlying materials. In contrast, acidic strippers employ a low pH composition, which can rapidly dissolve paints but may be harsher on sensitive surfaces. Both types are essential for effective paint removal, catering to different user needs depending on surface compatibility and removal efficiency.</p></p>
