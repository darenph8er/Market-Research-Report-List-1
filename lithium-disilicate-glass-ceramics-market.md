<p><p>Lithium disilicate glass ceramics are categorized into different market types based on their crystalline content and properties. High-Temperature (HT) ceramics offer superior strength and thermal stability for demanding applications. Medium-Temperature (MT) ceramics balance strength and aesthetic qualities, suitable for various dental restorations. Low-Temperature (LO) ceramics are ideal for applications requiring lower firing temperatures. Medium-Oxide (MO) ceramics provide enhanced optical properties. Other types may include specific formulations catering to niche applications, emphasizing versatility and performance in dental and industrial fields.</p></p>
