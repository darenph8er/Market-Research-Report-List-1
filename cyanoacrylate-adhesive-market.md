<p><p>Cyanoacrylate adhesives, known for fast curing and strong bonds, are categorized by their ester groups. Alkoxy-ethyl-based adhesives offer enhanced flexibility and moisture resistance, ideal for demanding environments. Ethyl-ester-based variants are widely used for general-purpose bonding, balancing strength and versatility. Methyl-ester-based adhesives provide rapid curing and high strength, suitable for rigid materials. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, from electronics to automotive, based on their chemical structure and performance characteristics, ensuring optimal adhesion for diverse applications.</p></p>
