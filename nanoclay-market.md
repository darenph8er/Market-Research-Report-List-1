<p><p>The nanoclay market comprises primarily two types: Kaolinite and Smectite. Kaolinite is known for its fine particle size and high purity, making it suitable for applications in ceramics, paints, and cosmetics. Smectite, characterized by its swelling properties, is commonly used in drilling fluids, cosmetics, and packaging industries. Both types enhance material properties, including barrier performance, thermal stability, and improved mechanical strength, driving their demand across various sectors such as construction, automotive, and packaging.</p></p>
