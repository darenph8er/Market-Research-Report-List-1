<p><p>Vehicle-mounted millimeter wave radar systems operate primarily at 24GHz and 77GHz frequencies, each serving distinct applications. In contrast, the 77GHz band supports long-range detection and advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS), enabling features like adaptive cruise control and automatic emergency braking.
