<p><p>Keyseating broaches are specialized tools used for shaping keyways and grooves in materials. The market can be divided into two main types: push type and pull type. Push type broaches are driven into the workpiece, creating the desired shape through a forward motion, making them suitable for softer materials. In contrast, pull type broaches are drawn through the material, which allows for greater control and is ideal for harder materials. Each type caters to specific applications and machining requirements in manufacturing.</p></p>
