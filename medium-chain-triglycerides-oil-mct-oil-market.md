C6-C8 consists of caprylic and capric acids, offering rapid absorption and immediate energy, making them popular in sports and weight management. C10-C12 contains capric and lauric acids, slower to metabolize, often used in cooking and dietary supplements for sustained energy and wellness.</p></p>
