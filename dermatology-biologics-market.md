<p><p>The dermatology biologics market includes various therapeutic categories targeting specific molecular pathways. Interleukin inhibitors focus on blocking interleukin proteins, which play a crucial role in inflammatory processes, thereby managing conditions such as psoriasis and atopic dermatitis. Tumor Necrosis Factor (TNF) inhibitors target TNF, a cytokine involved in systemic inflammation, also addressing autoimmune skin disorders. Both types of biologics offer targeted treatment options, improving efficacy and safety profiles compared to traditional therapies, and are integral to modern dermatology practices.</p></p>
