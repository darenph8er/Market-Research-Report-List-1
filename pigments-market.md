<p><p>The pigments market is broadly classified into inorganic and organic pigments. Inorganic pigments, derived from mineral sources, offer excellent stability and durability, commonly used in coatings, plastics, and construction materials. They include colors like titanium dioxide and iron oxide. Organic pigments, derived from carbon-based compounds, provide vibrant colors and high tinting strength, often used in printing inks, textiles, and coatings. Organic pigments can be more susceptible to fading but are favored for their brightness and versatility in various applications.</p></p>
