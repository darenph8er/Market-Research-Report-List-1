<p><p>The fire suppressant market encompasses various types, each tailored for specific applications. Foam fire suppressants create a barrier, effectively smothering flames while cooling the fire. Powder fire suppressants, versatile in nature, interrupt the chemical reaction of combustion. Carbon dioxide fire suppressants displace oxygen, making them ideal for electronic equipment fires. Wet chemical fire suppressants are specifically designed for kitchen fires involving oils and fats. Other fire suppressants may include water mist, halon alternatives, and encapsulating agents, catering to diverse fire scenarios.</p></p>
