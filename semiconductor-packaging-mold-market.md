<p><strong>The Semiconductor Packaging Mold Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transfer Molds</li><li>Compression Molds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor packaging mold market comprises mainly two types: transfer molds and compression molds. Transfer molds involve injecting molten molding compound into a pre-heated mold, ensuring precise filling and better control over the encapsulation process, which is ideal for complex designs. In contrast, compression molds rely on placing a predetermined amount of material into a mold cavity, then applying heat and pressure to shape it. This method is cost-effective for high-volume production and simpler designs, making both processes vital for semiconductor packaging.</p></p>
