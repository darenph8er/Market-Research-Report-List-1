<p><p>Plastic decorative laminates are categorized into two main types: low pressure and high pressure laminates. Low pressure decorative laminates are produced using less intense heat and pressure, making them lightweight and ideal for applications where flexibility and cost-effectiveness are priorities, such as furniture surfaces and cabinetry. In contrast, high pressure decorative laminates are manufactured under elevated pressure and temperature, resulting in a more durable, resistant product suitable for heavy-use surfaces like countertops and commercial spaces, offering enhanced longevity and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
