<p><strong>The UV-LED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV-A LED</li><li>UV-B LED</li><li>UV-C LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-LED market is categorized into three main types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C LEDs. UV-A LEDs emit wavelengths of 320-400 nm and are primarily used in curing applications and skin therapy. UV-B LEDs, with wavelengths of 280-320 nm, are critical for phototherapy and sterilization processes. UV-C LEDs, ranging from 200-280 nm, are highly effective for disinfection and germicidal applications, making them essential in water treatment, air purification, and surface sterilization. Each type serves distinct purposes across various industries.</p></p>
