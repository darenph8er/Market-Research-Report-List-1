<p><ul><li>Pervious Concrete</li><li>Porous Asphalt</li><li>Interlocking Concrete Pavers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrological pervious pavement market encompasses three primary types: pervious concrete, porous asphalt, and interlocking concrete pavers. Pervious concrete is formulated with larger aggregates to allow water infiltration, effectively managing stormwater. Porous asphalt features a similar design but utilizes asphalt for durability while enabling water drainage. Interlocking concrete pavers consist of modular units that form gaps for water absorption, promoting effective runoff management. These innovations play a crucial role in sustainable urban development, enhancing stormwater management and reducing flood risks.</p></p>
