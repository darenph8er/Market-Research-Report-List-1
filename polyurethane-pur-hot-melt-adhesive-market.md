<p><p>The polyurethane (PUR) hot melt adhesive market is divided into two main types: thermoplastic PUR hot-melt adhesives and reactive PUR hot-melt adhesives. Thermoplastic PUR adhesives offer ease of processing and quick setting, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility, such as packaging and textile bonding. In contrast, reactive PUR hot-melt adhesives undergo a curing process that improves their strength and moisture resistance, making them suitable for demanding applications in construction, automotive, and electronics industries.</p></p>
