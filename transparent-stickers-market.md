<p><p>Transparent stickers are adhesive labels made from clear materials, allowing the background to show through. In the market, they are available in two main types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided transparent stickers have adhesive on one side, ideal for surface applications like product labeling or decoration. Double-sided transparent stickers have adhesive on both sides, used for bonding two surfaces together, such as in mounting or crafting. Both types offer versatility, durability, and a seamless look, catering to various industries and creative needs.</p></p>
