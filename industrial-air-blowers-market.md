<p><p>The industrial air blowers market encompasses several key types: </p><p>1. **Centrifugal Blowers** use rotational energy to move air, providing high airflow with lower pressure. </p><p>2. **Roots Blowers** utilize a pair of meshing lobes for positive displacement, offering high pressure and efficiency for various applications. </p><p>3. **Screw Blowers** employ twin screws for air compression, providing smooth and continuous flow with reduced pulsation. </p><p>4. **Other Types** include axial blowers and regenerative blowers, catering to specific industry needs and varying operational requirements.</p></p>
