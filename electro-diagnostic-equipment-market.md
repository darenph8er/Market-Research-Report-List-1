<p><p>The electro-diagnostic equipment market encompasses various technologies used for assessing electrical activity in different body systems. Electromyogram (EMG) evaluates muscle electrical activity to diagnose neuromuscular disorders. Nerve Conduction Studies (NCS) assess nerve function by measuring signal speed. Electrocardiography (ECG) monitors heart activity, identifying cardiac conditions. Electroencephalography (EEG) records brain wave patterns, aiding in neurological diagnosis. Electroretinography (ERG) assesses retinal function, while Electrogastrography (EGG) monitors gastric electrical activity, providing insights into digestive health. Each type serves crucial diagnostic roles in healthcare.</p></p>
