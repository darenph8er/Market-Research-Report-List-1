<p><p>Pharmaceutical grade sorbitol is available in two primary forms: sorbitol solution and sorbitol crystals. Sorbitol solution is a clear, viscous liquid used as a humectant, sweetener, and stabilizer in various pharmaceutical formulations. Sorbitol crystals, on the other hand, are solid particles commonly used in solid dosage forms, offering benefits like low hygroscopicity and improved stability. Both types play crucial roles in formulating medications, offering benefits such as enhanced shelf life and optimal delivery of active ingredients.</p></p>
