<p><strong>The Industrial Crystallizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporative Crystallizers</li><li>Cooling Crystallizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial crystallizer market comprises two main types: evaporative and cooling crystallizers. Evaporative crystallizers operate by concentrating a solution through evaporation, leading to the formation of crystals as solvent is removed. This method is typically used for solutions with high solute concentrations. In contrast, cooling crystallizers work by lowering the temperature of a solution, promoting crystal growth as solubility decreases at lower temperatures. Both types are essential in various industries for separation and purification processes.</p></p>
