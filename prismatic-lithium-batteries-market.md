<p><p>Prismatic lithium batteries are categorized into various types based on their cathode materials. Lithium Cobalt Oxide (LiCoO2) offers high energy density, ideal for portable electronics. Lithium Manganese Oxide (LiMn2O4) provides better thermal stability and safety. Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt Oxide (NMC) balances energy density, power, and lifespan, making it popular in electric vehicles. Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4) features excellent thermal stability and safety but lower energy density. Lithium Nickel Cobalt Aluminum Oxide (NCA) excels in high energy applications, while Lithium Titanate (Li4Ti5O12) provides rapid charging capabilities.</p></p>
