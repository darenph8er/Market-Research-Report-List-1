<p><p>The Biologic and Medical Imaging Reagents Market includes several types of technologies. Nanoparticles are engineered particles used for drug delivery and imaging enhancements. Fluorescent proteins serve as markers for cellular processes, aiding in live-cell imaging. Fluorescent dyes and probes are used for staining and visualizing specific tissues or molecules. Radiopharmaceuticals combine imaging with therapeutic applications, utilizing radioactive isotopes for diagnostics. Quantum dots are nanoscale semiconductor particles that provide bright, tunable fluorescence for high-resolution imaging. Each type enhances visualization in biomedical research and clinical applications.</p></p>
