<p><p>The kidney/renal cancer drugs market encompasses several treatment types: targeted therapy, chemotherapy, and immunotherapy. Targeted therapy employs drugs that specifically attack cancer cells' unique characteristics, minimizing damage to normal cells. Chemotherapy utilizes cytotoxic agents to kill rapidly dividing cells, but can affect healthy tissue, leading to side effects. Immunotherapy harnesses the body's immune system to recognize and combat cancer cells, often resulting in longer-lasting responses. Each treatment approach offers distinct mechanisms and benefits in managing kidney cancer.</p></p>
