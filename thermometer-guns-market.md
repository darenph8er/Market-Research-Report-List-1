<p><p>The thermometer guns market is primarily divided into two types: non-contact and contact types. Non-contact thermometer guns utilize infrared technology to measure surface temperatures without direct contact, making them ideal for quick assessments and reducing the risk of contamination. In contrast, contact thermometer guns require physical interaction with the object or person being measured, providing accurate readings but requiring careful cleaning and handling. Both types serve critical roles in medical, industrial, and safety applications, especially in hygiene-sensitive environments.</p></p>
