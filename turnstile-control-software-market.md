<p><p>Turnstile control software can be categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and local-based solutions. Cloud-based turnstile control software is hosted on remote servers, allowing for easy access and management from anywhere, promoting scalability and reducing maintenance costs. This type often offers enhanced security but may involve higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance challenges, making the choice dependent on specific organizational needs.</p></p>
