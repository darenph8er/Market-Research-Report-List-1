<p><p>The agricultural limestone market primarily consists of three types: calcitic, dolomitic, and other forms. Calcitic limestone is rich in calcium carbonate, enhancing soil fertility and pH balance. Dolomitic limestone contains both calcium and magnesium carbonate, providing additional nutrients beneficial for crop growth. Other types may include liquid lime or gypsum, serving specific soil improvement needs. Each type addresses different agricultural requirements, influencing plant health and productivity, while promoting sustainable farming practices through effective soil management.</p></p>
