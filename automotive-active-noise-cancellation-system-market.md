<p><p>The automotive active noise cancellation market is divided into two main types: powertrain noise cancellation and road noise cancellation. Powertrain noise cancellation targets sounds generated by the engine and mechanical components, enhancing passenger comfort by reducing intrusive vibrations and noises. Conversely, road noise cancellation focuses on minimizing sounds that enter the cabin from the vehicle's interaction with the road surface, providing a quieter driving experience. Both types utilize advanced technologies to create counteracting sound waves, improving overall vehicle acoustics.</p></p>
