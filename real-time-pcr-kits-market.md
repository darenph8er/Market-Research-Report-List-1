<p><ul><li>One-step Real Time PCR</li><li>Two-step Real Time PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Real Time PCR kits market is segmented into One-step and Two-step Real Time PCR types. One-step Real Time PCR combines both reverse transcription and PCR amplification in a single reaction, enhancing convenience and efficiency, particularly for RNA analysis. In contrast, Two-step Real Time PCR involves separate reverse transcription followed by PCR amplification, providing greater flexibility and specificity for quantifying gene expression. These distinct methodologies cater to varying research needs and applications in diagnostics and biotechnology.</p></p>
