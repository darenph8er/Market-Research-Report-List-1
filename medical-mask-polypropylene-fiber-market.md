<p><p>The medical mask polypropylene fiber market can be categorized based on melt flow index (MFI) into two types: ≤35 g/10 min and >35 g/10 min. Fibers with an MFI of ≤35 g/10 min are characterized by higher viscosity, making them suitable for applications requiring durability and filtration efficiency. In contrast, fibers with an MFI of >35 g/10 min are more fluid and easier to process, often used for lightweight masks that prioritize breathability and comfort without compromising protection.</p></p>
