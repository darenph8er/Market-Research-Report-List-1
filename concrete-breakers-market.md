<p><p>The concrete breakers market consists of three primary types: pneumatic breakers, hydraulic breakers, and petrol breakers. Pneumatic breakers are powered by compressed air, making them lightweight and effective for various demolition tasks. Hydraulic breakers utilize hydraulic power for greater efficiency and force, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Petrol breakers are engine-powered, offering portability and flexibility for remote job sites. Each type caters to different project needs, balancing power, weight, and mobility for effective concrete demolition.</p></p>
