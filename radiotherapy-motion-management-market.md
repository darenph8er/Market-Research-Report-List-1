<p><p>Radiotherapy motion management encompasses strategies to mitigate patient movement during treatment. Spirometric motion management utilizes respiratory monitoring to synchronize treatment with breathing patterns, ensuring precise targeting of tumors. Intrafraction radiotherapy motion management focuses on real-time tracking of patient movement during treatment sessions, adjusting delivery accordingly to maintain accuracy. Other methods include various imaging and mechanical systems designed to stabilize patients or adjust treatment plans based on detected motion, ultimately enhancing the efficacy and safety of radiotherapy.</p></p>
