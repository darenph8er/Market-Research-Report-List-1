<p><p>Industrial tilt sensors are essential devices used to measure the angle of inclination or tilt in various applications. The market is primarily segmented into single-axis and dual-axis sensors. Single-axis tilt sensors measure tilt in one direction, making them suitable for applications like detecting vehicle inclination. In contrast, dual-axis tilt sensors measure tilt in two perpendicular directions, providing a comprehensive view of an object's orientation. This versatility makes dual-axis sensors ideal for complex machinery and automation systems where precise positioning is critical.</p></p>
