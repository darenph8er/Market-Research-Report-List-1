<p><p>Self-aligning ball bearings are a type of rolling element bearing designed to accommodate misalignment between the shaft and housing. They feature two rows of balls and a common spherical outer raceway, allowing them to self-align when subjected to misalignment or shaft deflection. This functionality makes them ideal for applications in heavy machinery and automotive industries, where ease of maintenance and reliability are critical.</p><p>The Self-aligning Ball Bearing Market is experiencing significant growth attributed to increasing demand from various sectors, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial machinery.
