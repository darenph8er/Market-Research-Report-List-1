<p><p>The plastic hot and cold pipes market comprises various types suited for plumbing and heating applications. Cross-Linked Polyethylene (PEX) offers flexibility and resistance to scale and chlorine. Polyethylene Raised Temperatures (PE-RT) is designed for higher temperature resistance, ideal for heating systems. Polypropylene Random Copolymer (PPR) is known for its strength, chemical resistance, and durability. Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride (C-PVC) is effective for hot water applications due to its heat resistance, while Polybutylene (PB) is known for its lightweight and easy installation, though it’s less common now.</p></p>
