<p><p>The Human Amniotic Membrane (AM) market consists of two primary types: cryopreserved and dehydrated amniotic membrane. Cryopreserved amniotic membrane is preserved at low temperatures to retain its biological properties, making it suitable for various surgical applications and wound healing. In contrast, dehydrated amniotic membrane is processed to remove moisture, extending its shelf life and ease of handling. Both types are utilized in regenerative medicine, ranging from ophthalmology to orthopedics, due to their ability to promote healing and reduce scarring.</p></p>
