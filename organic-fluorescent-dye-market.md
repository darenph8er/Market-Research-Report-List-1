<p><p>The Organic Fluorescent Dye market includes types like Fluorescein and Rhodamine dyes, widely used in biological imaging, diagnostics, and research. Fluorescein dyes are known for their bright green fluorescence, high water solubility, and applications in medical diagnostics and flow cytometry. Rhodamine dyes emit red to orange fluorescence, offering superior photostability and versatility in labeling and microscopy. Both dye types are essential in life sciences, with Rhodamine often preferred for deeper tissue imaging due to its longer emission wavelengths.</p></p>
