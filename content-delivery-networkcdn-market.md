<p><p>A Content Delivery Network (CDN) is a system of distributed servers that deliver web content and services to users based on their geographic location. By caching content closer to users, CDNs enhance the speed and reliability of web applications, improving user experience and reducing latency. The demand for CDNs has surged due to the increasing consumption of digital content, the rise in online streaming services, and the need for improved website performance and security. </p><p>The CDN market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period, driven by the proliferation of mobile devices and the continuous growth of e-commerce. Businesses are increasingly adopting CDN solutions to ensure high availability and rapid delivery of content, particularly in times of high traffic. Additionally, advancements in technologies such as edge computing and IoT are spurring new applications for CDN services, particularly in sectors like gaming, entertainment, and online education. Increasing concerns over data security and privacy are also prompting organizations to invest in secure CDN solutions that offer enhanced protection against DDoS attacks and other cyber threats. Overall, the CDN landscape is evolving, integrating innovative features to cater to diverse user needs.</p></p>
