<p><p>An enteral feeding pump is a medical device designed to deliver nutrition directly to the gastrointestinal tract of patients who cannot consume food orally. They offer precise control over the flow rate of enteral formula, ensuring patients receive consistent and adequate nutrition.</p><p>The Enteral Feeding Pump Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.10% during the forecast period, driven by an increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, advancements in pump technology, and rising awareness regarding enteral nutrition. Manufacturers are focusing on developing smart pumps equipped with advanced features like connectivity and monitoring capabilities, enhancing patient outcomes.
