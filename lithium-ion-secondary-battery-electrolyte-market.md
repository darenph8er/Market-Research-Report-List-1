<p><p>The lithium-ion secondary battery electrolyte market comprises two main types: liquid and solid electrolytes. Liquid electrolytes, typically composed of lithium salts dissolved in organic solvents, offer high ionic conductivity and ease of manufacturing, making them prevalent in consumer electronics and electric vehicles. Solid electrolytes, on the other hand, are made from solid materials that enhance safety by reducing leakage risks and enabling higher operating temperatures. They show promise for next-generation batteries with improved energy density and longevity, driving innovation in the market.</p></p>
