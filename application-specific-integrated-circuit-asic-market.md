<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Application Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Custom Design ASIC</li><li>Semi-custom Design ASIC (Standard Cell Based ASIC and Gate Array Based ASIC)</li><li>Programmable ASIC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ASIC market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Full Custom Design ASIC**: Tailored specifically for a single application, offering superior performance and efficiency but requires significant time and cost for design. </p><p>2. **Semi-custom Design ASIC**: Includes Standard Cell Based ASICs, which use pre-designed cells for flexibility, and Gate Array Based ASICs, which allow customization after fabrication, balancing cost and performance. </p><p>3. **Programmable ASIC**: Offers reconfigurability post-manufacturing, allowing for adaptability across various applications, making them versatile for evolving needs.</p></p>
