<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) Injector market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-port Midspan and Multi-port Midspan. Single-port Midspan injectors provide Power over Ethernet to a single device, making them ideal for smaller installations or specific peripherals. In contrast, Multi-port Midspan injectors distribute power to multiple devices simultaneously, suitable for larger networks requiring numerous powered devices. Both types enable efficient power distribution alongside data transfer, simplifying network cabling and enhancing operational flexibility in various settings.</p></p>
