<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) systems optimize engine performance by adjusting valve timing for improved efficiency, power, and emissions. The market includes **Vane-type VVT**, which uses a rotor and stator mechanism for precise, continuous timing adjustments, offering smooth operation and compact design. **Gear-type VVT** employs gear-driven mechanisms for robust and reliable timing control, often preferred in high-performance or heavy-duty applications. Both systems enhance engine dynamics, with vane-type focusing on precision and gear-type emphasizing durability, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
