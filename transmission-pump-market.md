<p><strong>The Transmission Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed displacement pump</li><li>Variable displacement pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transmission pump market is primarily divided into two types: fixed displacement pumps and variable displacement pumps. Fixed displacement pumps deliver a constant volume of fluid with each cycle, making them simple and reliable for applications requiring steady flow. In contrast, variable displacement pumps adjust their output based on system demands, optimizing efficiency and performance in dynamic conditions. These distinct types cater to varying operational needs, influencing their adoption in automotive, industrial, and hydraulic applications, enhancing overall system functionality.</p></p>
